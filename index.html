Our History

Ali Baba's Fabulous Donuts was the dream business of our father, Ali Baba, who moved from Cairo, Egypt during the revolution to the lovely town of Dearborn, Michigan. Donuts were one of his all-time favorite sweets, and one day, he decided to risk it all and open up shop, creating the titular business.

New weekly products was Ali Baba's idea of innovation. Allured and excited by the idea of new donuts that would be rotated into stock, he believed it would prove a staple for his business and attract interest in the community. And so it has! People flocked to the store from day one, interested in finding what donuts would be up for sale every passing week, and many found it a great place for a quick morning dessert and for parlour discussions with friends and fellows.

We're still going strong at 50 years, and we mean to stay for another 50! Through your patronage, we relive the joys of our father Ali Baba and his passion for donut-baking. To you all, we extend our deepest thank-you's.
